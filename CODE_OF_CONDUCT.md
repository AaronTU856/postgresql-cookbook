# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a welcoming, respectful, and useful learning space for developers at different levels of experience.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give feedback that helps the work improve.
- Keep discussions focused on learning, correctness, and maintainability.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behaviour
- Dismissive responses to beginner questions
- Spam or off-topic promotion

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or pull requests that do not meet these standards. Repeated or serious violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.
